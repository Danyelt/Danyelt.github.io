iPad Accessibilty

"You're not disabled by the disabilites you have, you are able by the abilities you have.".

-Oscar Pistorius

Accessibility which is referred to as inclusivity ensures individuals, who have a disability are still able to be included in the classroom. According to the Australian Bureau of statics, In 2009, there were an estimated 292,600 children with disability attending school in Australia. These children were aged between 5-20 years and represented approximately one in twelve of all Australian children attending school in this age group.
